# Create a Token with Custom Parameters

Learn how to customize your bToken's parameters for your specific use case and community needs.

## Understanding Custom Parameters

### Why Customize?

While Baseline provides sensible defaults, customizing parameters allows you to:
- **Optimize for your community**: Tailor to your specific user base
- **Achieve specific goals**: Design for particular use cases
- **Differentiate your token**: Create unique value propositions
- **Adapt to market conditions**: Respond to changing environments

### Key Parameters to Consider

#### Token Economics
- **Initial Supply**: Total tokens minted at launch
- **Decimals**: Precision of token amounts (usually 18)
- **Distribution**: How tokens are allocated initially

#### BLV Configuration
- **Initial Price Floor**: Starting minimum price
- **Floor Growth Rate**: How quickly the floor can increase
- **Liquidity Depth**: Amount of initial liquidity provided

#### Trading Parameters
- **Spread Configuration**: Bid/ask spread settings
- **Fee Structure**: Trading fees and their distribution
- **Volume Limits**: Maximum trade sizes

## Step-by-Step Customization

### Step 1: Define Your Vision

Before setting parameters, clarify:
- **Target Audience**: Who will use your token?
- **Use Case**: What problem does it solve?
- **Growth Strategy**: How will you attract users?
- **Competitive Advantage**: What makes it unique?

### Step 2: Choose Your Parameters

#### Conservative Approach
- **Low Initial Supply**: 100,000 - 1,000,000 tokens
- **High Price Floor**: $0.50 - $1.00 minimum
- **Low Fees**: 1% trading fee
- **Gradual Growth**: Slow BLV increases

#### Aggressive Approach
- **High Initial Supply**: 10,000,000+ tokens
- **Low Price Floor**: $0.01 - $0.10 minimum
- **Higher Fees**: 2-3% trading fee
- **Rapid Growth**: Fast BLV increases

#### Balanced Approach
- **Medium Supply**: 1,000,000 - 10,000,000 tokens
- **Moderate Floor**: $0.10 - $0.50 minimum
- **Standard Fees**: 1.5% trading fee
- **Adaptive Growth**: BLV adjusts to market conditions

### Step 3: Test Your Configuration

#### Simulation Tools
- Use Baseline's parameter simulator
- Test different scenarios
- Validate assumptions
- Get community feedback

#### Risk Assessment
- **Liquidity Risk**: Will there be enough trading?
- **Volatility Risk**: How stable will the price be?
- **Adoption Risk**: Will users embrace the token?

## Advanced Customization Options

### Dynamic Parameters
- **Adaptive Fees**: Fees that change based on volume
- **Time-based Adjustments**: Parameters that evolve over time
- **Performance Triggers**: Changes based on specific metrics

### Governance Integration
- **Community Voting**: Let holders decide on parameter changes
- **Proposal System**: Structured process for updates
- **Emergency Controls**: Quick response to market events

### Cross-Token Features
- **Token Pairs**: Relationships with other bTokens
- **Basket Tokens**: Multi-token portfolios
- **Index Tokens**: Exposure to multiple assets

## Best Practices

### Parameter Selection
1. **Start Simple**: Begin with basic customization
2. **Test Thoroughly**: Use testnets and simulations
3. **Document Decisions**: Record your reasoning
4. **Plan for Evolution**: Design for future changes

### Community Involvement
- **Gather Input**: Survey your target audience
- **Explain Trade-offs**: Help users understand choices
- **Iterate Responsively**: Adjust based on feedback
- **Maintain Transparency**: Keep community informed

### Risk Management
- **Conservative Estimates**: Plan for worst-case scenarios
- **Gradual Rollout**: Implement changes incrementally
- **Monitoring Systems**: Track parameter effectiveness
- **Exit Strategies**: Plan for parameter adjustments

## Common Customization Patterns

### Community Tokens
- **Low Fees**: Encourage active trading
- **High Staking Rewards**: Reward long-term holders
- **Governance Rights**: Give community control

### Utility Tokens
- **Usage-based Fees**: Tie fees to platform usage
- **Staking Requirements**: Require staking for benefits
- **Burn Mechanisms**: Reduce supply over time

### Investment Tokens
- **High Price Floors**: Provide stability
- **Low Volatility**: Minimize price swings
- **Dividend Distribution**: Share profits with holders
